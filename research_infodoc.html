The DIYer's Biggest Hurdles

While enthusiasm is high, DIY automotive work is fraught with challenges. These common frustrations represent the most significant opportunities for providing value through digital tools and expert guidance. Successfully addressing these pain points is key to building trust and loyalty.

Top Reported DIY Frustrations

This chart highlights the most frequently cited problems faced by DIY enthusiasts. Electrical issues and diagnosing "check engine" lights are dominant pain points, indicating a critical need for reliable diagnostic tools that can simplify these complex tasks.

The "Guidance Gap" Problem

This donut chart visualizes the "guidance gap": the large percentage of DIYers who start a project but eventually require expert help due to unforeseen complexity or safety concerns. This gap is the prime conversion opportunity for paid assistance.
